GET TO KNOW ABOUT PROJECT GLOBAL CURE

Transforming Healthcare In Every Possible Way.

Project Global Cure is a Humanity Welfare Council initiative that aims to transform the health care sector in India by providing necessary funds to people in urgent need of medical assistance and advancing the medical system with increased accessibility. Our motto is to transform our health system and make medical care available for the general public without any burden of extra cost. Access to healthcare is a basic human right that every citizen should be able to afford. Our team comprises highly driven and committed individuals dedicated to creating a developed and healthy world. And in our race towards a developed India, the health sector is our priority. Our initiative symbolizes our philanthropist nature, and we have been making a profound difference in the lives of many. The healthcare system in our country is fighting various problems, including the insufficient number of institutions and short-staffed human resources. A few people get expensive, world-class care, while a large part of the population doesn't even get the primary quality care. Over 60% of our population pays out of pocket for healthcare, driving millions into poverty.


A healthy human is the best resource for any nation. They are the basis of the development of a country. Only if a person is physically and mentally fit, he thinks beyond his zone and works for the upliftment of the nation. As the saying goes, a healthy body is a temple where a healthy mind resides. We focus on creating a strong, robust and healthy environment as a progressive approach towards the developing world. We strive to provide medical access for every individual and build a disease-free human race. Therefore, we are creating a broader platform where everyone can easily reach in case of a medical emergency without any unnecessary formalities and delayed time in attending to their medical concerns. On this platform, we also aim to unite the medical researchers, medical institutions, government and non-government bodies under one roof to attain solutions to globally arising medical concerns. We are also working on creating advanced and high medical solutions at affordable costs. We maintain transparency regarding our daily fund operations and assistance services. However, we hold ourselves liable to our trusted donors and people who approach us for help.

Your time and skills could help us in saving a precious life!

OUR MISSION AND VISION


Making healthcare accessible to all and transforming health sector to make India healthy by 2030

Mission


We aim to create innovative technology and finance solutions to empower Indians concerning healthcare. We create a system to raise funds for medical emergencies, critical illnesses, and a range of social and personal causes. Lakhs have used our platform to change lives through timely contributions, fundraisers and auctions.

vision


We envision a country where medical care is available and accessible for every citizen without any burden of extra costs. We aim to reconstruct the healthcare system in India by providing necessary funds to people in desperate need of medical assistance and bringing advancement in the medical system.
